The Creator

All of our products are hand made by Beatrice Galindo (Bea). She has been crocheting for more than 30 years. Bea was originally taught to crochet by her mother Sally. In 2015, she had a granddaughter and taught herself how to make custome outfits like the pocahontas holloween costume you see on the Home page. Crocheting is something that Bea loves to do. It brings her joy to make your order so bring on the request!
